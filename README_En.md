## üöÄ What is RAMTN?

**RAMTN** is a novel open-source framework for **Human-AI Cognitive Symbiosis**. Its core innovation is the **Plug-and-Play Cognitive Framework (PPCF)** ‚Äì a structured, computable package that can be extracted from expert reasoning processes and loaded into any RAMTN instance. This transforms AI from a passive tool into an active **thinking partner** that can reason with the logic of domain experts.

Simply put, RAMTN enables **cognitive transfer**: it can "learn" how a master investor, a senior doctor, or an expert teacher thinks, and then empower anyone to apply that same structured reasoning to their own challenges.

> **Origins**: This project is the result of a 6-month independent exploration by an undergraduate student, conceived and built through deep "meta-interactions" with large language models, without formal supervision or institutional support. It is now fully open-sourced to advance the vision of **cognitive equity**.

## üîç Core Idea: From Dual Black Boxes to a "Functional White Box"

Current human-AI collaboration is limited by two fundamental opacities:
1.  **The Human Cognitive Black Box**: Expert intuition, strategic judgment, and tacit knowledge are difficult to articulate and transfer.
2.  **The AI Computational Black Box**: Model decisions are often untraceable and unaccountable, creating trust barriers.

RAMTN introduces a paradigm shift through **structured meta-interaction**. It distills human "hot cognition" (expert intuition) into reusable, explicit "cold cognition" frameworks while making AI's "cold cognition" adaptable to human context and values. The interaction protocol itself becomes a **verifiable, auditable Functional White Box**.

## üõ† How It Works: The RAMTN Architecture

RAMTN operates through a recursive, three-layer **Constructor-Critic-Observer** loop, guided by the loaded Pluggable Cognitive Framework.

1.  **Extraction Mode**: Input expert narratives or case studies. RAMTN engages in recursive adversarial dialogue to isolate core decision rules, constraints, and heuristics, outputting a structured PCF.
2.  **Enhancement Mode**: Input a new user's problem. RAMTN loads a relevant PCF (e.g., a diagnostic framework) and guides its analysis, producing expert-style reasoning tailored to the user's context, with outputs classified by confidence (**Confident, Speculative, Unknown**).

This creates a closed loop: **Expertise ‚Üí Structured Framework ‚Üí Augmented Decision Support**.

## üìà Why It Matters: Use Cases & Vision

RAMTN is designed as a **general-purpose cognitive augmentation platform**. Its initial proofs-of-concept span multiple high-stakes domains:

*   **Investment**: Extracting Buffett's capital allocation logic to analyze modern tech stocks.
*   **Healthcare**: Distilling a tertiary hospital diagnostician's framework to support grassroots clinicians with limited resources.
*   **Education**: Capturing a master teacher's pedagogical intuition to create adaptive lesson plans for diverse classrooms.

**The long-term vision is a Cognitive Commons**: a shared repository of vetted, interoperable cognitive frameworks that make high-level expertise a scalable public resource, reducing cognitive inequality.
